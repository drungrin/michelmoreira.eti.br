## Problem

Technical documentation automation at Techne: a processing job that took days.

## Approach

I designed a serverless, event-driven architecture: AWS Step Functions orchestrating Amazon Bedrock (Claude Sonnet), with RAG over pgvector on RDS PostgreSQL.

## Technology

AWS Step Functions, Amazon Bedrock (Claude Sonnet), RAG, pgvector, RDS PostgreSQL, serverless architecture.

## Result

The processing job went from days down to under an hour.
